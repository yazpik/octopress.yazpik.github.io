Who Am I ?

My name is Francisco Moctezuma, I’m just another computer guy who loves to work and play with OpenSource software and cloud. I define myself as a Linux enthusiast, I truly enjoy learning new things everyday basically self-studying, I’m currently working as OpenStack Administrator for Rackspace in San Antonio Texas. Born and raised in the beautiful state of Veracruz, Mexico, Living the life of a sysadmin who also loves good coffee, tacos, handcrafted beer, adventure riding, and hangout with my family.
