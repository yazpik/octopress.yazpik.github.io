2015 Challenge Accepted

Jan 1st, 2015 | Comments

It doesn’t matter how many roadblocks are out there, I know is going to be fun!

Photo was taken during my first ride of the year, I woke up early in the morning while everybody was sleeping after new years dinner, took my motorcycle (Yamaha XT660Z Tenere) baptized as “XamanEk” and rode around 80km, 30% pavement 70% offroad on an exploratory route, from my wife’s hometown to a Geothermal zone near to Tinajitas, Veracruz, It was not hard to find the place even its not in a map, just took me one stop asking people and rest was purely joy, I rode between hills, without a GPS (currently evaluating to get one), but at the end this is part of the fun of Adventure riding!

After a dip on crystal clear warm waters provided by mother earth, rode back with XamanEk just in time to had a lunch with the family.

2015 here we go!
